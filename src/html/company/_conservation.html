Commitment to Conservation

Africa Wild Life Explorer is dedicated to sustainable tourism and the preservation of Africa's wildlife and ecosystems. We believe in responsible safari practices, ensuring minimal impact on wildlife and habitats. Our guides are trained to educate guests on conservation while maintaining a respectful distance from animals.

  • Supporting Local Communities

    We partner with community-run lodges and camps, providing sustainable income sources

  • Anti-Poaching Efforts

    Collaborating with local units, we combat illegal wildlife trade and contribute resources to protect endangered species

  • Maasai Mara Wildlife Conservancies

    We contribute to conservancies, vital for wildlife corridors and habitat protection

  • Lion Guardians

    Supporting the Lion Guardians program, we aid in lion protection and reducing human-wildlife conflict
