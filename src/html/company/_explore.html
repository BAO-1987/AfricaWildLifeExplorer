Unveiling Africa Wild Life Explorer

Experience Africa's Beauty and Conservation with Us

  • Africa Wild Life Explorer was born from a shared passion for Africa's wildlife and a deep-seated commitment to sustainable tourism. Founded by a group of wildlife enthusiasts, our story began with a vision to create more than just a safari company - we aimed to be stewards of conservation and purveyors of unforgettable adventures.

    Our mission is to provide exceptional safari experiences that not only immerse our guests in the beauty of Africa's landscapes and wildlife but also contribute to the preservation of its natural wonders. We believe that every safari should leave a positive impact, supporting local communities and wildlife conservation efforts.

  • What sets Africa Wild Life Explorer apart is our dedication to personalized experiences. We focus on small-group tours to ensure intimate encounters with wildlife, guided by experts who share their knowledge and passion for conservation. Every journey with us is an opportunity to not only witness Africa's magnificence but also to actively contribute to its preservation.

    Embark on a journey with Africa Wild Life Explorer and discover the heart of Africa's wilderness. Our safari tours offer more than just wildlife sightings; they offer a profound connection to the land, its people, and its wildlife. Whether you dream of tracking the "Big Five" in the savannahs of the Serengeti, gliding through the waterways of the Okavango Delta, or trekking to see mountain gorillas in Rwanda, we have a safari adventure for you.
