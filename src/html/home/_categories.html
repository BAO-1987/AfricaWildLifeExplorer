Destination Categories

Explore Africa's Regions

Discover the diverse regions of Africa, each offering a unique tapestry of wildlife, landscapes, and cultural experiences. Whether you seek the dramatic savannas of East Africa, the pristine wilderness of Southern Africa, or the dense jungles of Central Africa, Africa Wild Life Explorer has a safari adventure for every explorer.

  • East Africa (Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda)

    Embark on a journey through East Africa, home to some of the world's most iconic safari destinations. In Kenya, witness the Great Migration in Masai Mara or trek through the lush forests of Mount Kenya. Tanzania beckons with the vast Serengeti and the majestic Mount Kilimanjaro. Explore Uganda's diverse landscapes, from the gorilla-filled jungles of Bwindi Impenetrable National Park to the tranquil shores of Lake Victoria.

  • Southern Africa (South Africa, Botswana, Zambia)

    Southern Africa offers a wealth of safari experiences, from the renowned Kruger National Park in South Africa to the Okavango Delta in Botswana, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Zambia's South Luangwa National Park is a paradise for walking safaris, while Victoria Falls captivates with its thundering beauty. Immerse yourself in the rich biodiversity and stunning landscapes of Southern Africa.

  • Central Africa (Rwanda, Congo, Central African Republic)

    Venture into the heart of Africa with Central Africa's hidden gems. Rwanda's Volcanoes National Park is famous for its mountain gorillas, offering a rare and intimate wildlife encounter. The Congo Basin in the Republic of Congo is a haven for forest elephants and western lowland gorillas. In the Central African Republic, explore pristine wilderness and encounter a mosaic of wildlife in Dzanga-Sangha National Park.
